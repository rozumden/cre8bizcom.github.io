Why creativity

You don't have to be a Leonardo to sketch out an idea.

There are many who like to draw and sketch. Some love the experience of designing a piece of graphics. Unfortunately, those who only use computer programs are missing out — big time — on developing their own design skills.

You don't have to be a Leonardo to sketch out an idea and develop it. A designer, after all, is not an artist. He is a problem solver who uses art to provide a visual solution to a problem.

Many captains of industry have constantly said how creative thinking and innovation are exactly what's needed to build future prosperity.
