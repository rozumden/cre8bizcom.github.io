Coaching & mentoring

Get more from the people you already have.

This is the pool of skills required for giving performance feedback, delegating, motivating and energising performance. Coaching asks leaders to be flexible — managing individuals with a variety of needs, skills and abilities. Contrary to what some managers assume, one-size-fits-all solutions do not apply to people.

Mentoring is one of the coaching skills — and a significant one. It promotes self-development and self-sufficiency, helping team members focus and be guided to do things for themselves.

In a nutshell, coaching and mentoring employees at all levels lets an organisation achieve more with existing resources. It goes a long way to retaining quality staff and adding value to the business.
