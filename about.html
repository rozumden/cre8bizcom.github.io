Four decades of communication, in service of people.

Creative Business Communications is built on a simple belief: the future belongs to those who can connect, express and persuade — and those skills can be practised.

The founder

Started in Cairo, 2005 — by Nigel Richards.

By the time he founded Creative Business Communications, Nigel Richards had already spent nearly 25 years as a designer, and later creative director, with leading international advertising agencies — with extensive experience in publishing and public relations besides.

His assignments took him around the world: launching new product brands and setting up agency offices in new markets. As more and more new employees arrived without the skills those markets demanded, he moved into training, where the demand was great.

Today he helps high-school students prepare for their maturita exams, guides university students toward the world of work, and mentors executives who want to develop professionally. The challenges facing young people ahead call for those with experience to support them — as much as possible.
